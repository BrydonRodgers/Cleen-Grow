Our Story

Born on the West Coast

CLEENGROW CO (PTY) LTD was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: to provide the Saldanha Bay area with professional, reliable, and affordable cleaning and transport services.

Serving the communities of Saldanha, Vredenburg, and Vredendal, we have built our reputation on doing the job right — every time. Whether it's a pressure-washed driveway, a spotless exterior wall, or a furniture move handled with care, CLEENGROW delivers.

We are CIPC registered (2024/597875/07) and fully compliant, giving our clients the peace of mind that comes with working with a legitimate, professional business.
