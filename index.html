FAQ New

Common questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Do you bring your own equipment? +
Yes — we bring all professional-grade pressure washing and cleaning equipment. You don't need to provide anything.
How do I pay? +
We accept EFT (bank transfer) and cash. Payment is due on completion of the job. We'll send you a simple invoice via WhatsApp.
How far in advance must I book? +
We try to accommodate last-minute bookings, but we recommend booking at least 2–3 days in advance to secure your preferred time slot.
Do you work on weekends? +
Yes! We work Monday to Saturday. WhatsApp us to check weekend availability for your area.
What areas do you cover for transport? +
We cover Saldanha, Vredenburg, and Vredendal as our primary areas. We also do longer trips (e.g. Cape Town airport) — just ask for a quote.
Is CLEENGROW CO a registered business? +
Yes — we are registered with the CIPC (Companies and Intellectual Property Commission). Reg: 2024/597875/07. Director: Jonathan Ray Jones.
